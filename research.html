Our Research

🧬

Protein Folding & Misfolding

We investigate the molecular mechanisms of protein folding in the endoplasmic reticulum and how misfolding leads to neurodegenerative diseases such as Alzheimer's and Parkinson's.

🔬

Ubiquitin-Proteasome System

Our lab studies the regulation of the ubiquitin-proteasome pathway, focusing on E3 ligases and deubiquitinases that control protein degradation under stress conditions.

🌡️

Heat Shock Response

We explore how cells activate heat shock proteins (HSPs) to maintain proteostasis during thermal or oxidative stress, and how this pathway is dysregulated in cancer.

🧫

Autophagy & Lysosomal Degradation

We aim to understand selective autophagy pathways that clear aggregated proteins and damaged organelles, with implications for aging and metabolic disorders.
